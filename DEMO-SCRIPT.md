## RECORDING TIPS

- **Phone vertical** for IG/TikTok, **horizontal** for YouTube
- **Real screen recording** — don't fake the demo, do it live
- **Have Kyle Smith's job pre-loaded** — but click through it live
- **Cut hard between scenes** — no slow transitions
- **Add captions** — most people watch muted
- **Background music:** subtle tech/cinematic, low volume
- **Hook in first 3 seconds** — "five grand on the table" is the hook
