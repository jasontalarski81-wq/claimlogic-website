Partner / Affiliate Program Terms

Effective: April 30, 2026 · Version 1.0 · Claim Logic LLC
The short version: Refer paying customers to ClaimLogic, earn 20% of the monthly recurring revenue for the lifetime of each customer (so long as they remain active and paying). Sales partners are independent contractors. No exclusivity. ClaimLogic owns all software and intellectual property.

1. Eligibility

Anyone 18 or older with the legal capacity to enter contracts may apply. Eligibility is at our sole discretion. We reserve the right to reject or remove any applicant or partner without cause.

2. Independent Contractor

You are an independent contractor. You are not an employee, agent, joint venturer, or partner of Claim Logic LLC. You have no authority to bind ClaimLogic, make representations on its behalf, or sign contracts in its name. You are responsible for your own taxes, expenses, and benefits.

3. Commission

20% of monthly recurring subscription revenue (MRR) from each customer you successfully refer who: (a) signs up using your unique referral link or referral code; (b) completes payment for an active subscription; (c) is not an existing or recently lapsed ClaimLogic customer.

Commission is paid for the lifetime of each referred customer's active paying status. If the customer cancels, downgrades, refunds, charges back, or stops paying, commission stops accruing for that customer. Commission resumes if and when the customer pays again.

4. Payment

  • Commissions are calculated monthly based on cleared (non-refunded, non-charged-back) revenue collected by ClaimLogic in the prior month.
  • Commissions are paid by the 15th of the following month via Stripe Connect, ACH, or check at our discretion.
  • Minimum payout: $50. Balances under $50 roll forward to future months.
  • Charges that are refunded, charged back, or determined fraudulent are clawed back from current or future commission balances.
  • You are responsible for tax reporting on all commissions earned. ClaimLogic will issue Form 1099-NEC where required by U.S. tax law.

5. Self-Referrals & Fraud

You may not refer yourself, immediate family members, or accounts you control. Fraudulent referrals — including spam, paid-search bidding on ClaimLogic trademarks, fake signups, or any deceptive tactic — result in immediate termination, forfeiture of all unpaid commissions, and clawback of paid commissions.

6. Marketing Conduct

You must:

  • Comply with CAN-SPAM, TCPA, GDPR (where applicable), and all anti-spam laws;
  • Disclose your affiliate relationship in promotional materials per FTC rules;
  • Not impersonate ClaimLogic or claim to be an employee, public adjuster, attorney, or licensed insurance professional unless you actually are;
  • Not make any guarantee, promise, or claim about insurance outcomes, recovery amounts, or supplement approval rates;
  • Not bid on or use the trademarks "ClaimLogic", "Claim Logic", or "getclaimlogic" in paid advertising without prior written approval;
  • Not run promotional content that is deceptive, defamatory, or otherwise prohibited by our Acceptable Use Policy.

7. Intellectual Property

ClaimLogic owns all rights to the software, brand, logos, marketing assets, and Service. You receive a limited, revocable license to use approved promotional assets while in good standing. You may not copy, modify, or replicate Service workflows, prompts, decision logic, or supplement-generation processes for any purpose.

8. Non-Circumvention

You shall not solicit, contact, or attempt to do business with ClaimLogic's customers, vendors, employees, contractors, or partners (including referrals you originated) for the purpose of avoiding ClaimLogic, building a competing service, or moving them to a competing product. This obligation continues for two (2) years after termination of your participation in the program.

9. Non-Compete (Limited)

While participating in the program and for twelve (12) months after termination, you shall not promote, develop, or actively sell any product that is substantially similar to ClaimLogic and targets roofing, restoration, or insurance-supplement automation as its primary use case.

10. Confidentiality

You will receive non-public information including pricing tiers, prospect data, internal sales materials, performance metrics, and business strategy. You shall hold all such information in strict confidence, use it only for promoting ClaimLogic, and not disclose it to any third party. Confidentiality survives termination indefinitely for trade secrets and for three (3) years for other Confidential Information.

11. Term & Termination

Either party may terminate participation at any time with 7 days' notice. ClaimLogic may terminate immediately for breach, fraud, or misconduct. Upon termination, accrued unpaid commissions for cleared revenue remain payable, but no further commissions accrue after the termination date — even if a customer continues paying. (Exception: at our sole discretion, we may continue paying commissions on customers you originated for a transition period.)

12. No Guarantee

ClaimLogic does not guarantee any minimum income, lead volume, conversion rate, or commission. Earnings depend on your effort and skill. Past performance of other partners is not a reliable predictor of your results.

13. Modifications

We may update these Partner Terms (including commission rates, eligibility rules, and payout terms) on 30 days' written notice. Continued participation after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

14. Indemnification

You shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Claim Logic LLC from any third-party claim arising from your marketing activity, representations, breach of these terms, or violation of law.

15. Governing Law

Missouri law governs. Disputes are exclusively resolved in St. Louis County, Missouri courts. Jury trial waived.
