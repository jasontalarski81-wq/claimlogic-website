Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2026 · Version 1.0 · Claim Logic LLC,
The short version: We collect data needed to run ClaimLogic. We don't sell your data. Your insurance scope and customer information stay private to your account. We use industry-standard security and comply with applicable privacy laws.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy explains how Claim Logic LLC ("ClaimLogic", "we", "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our platform, websites, applications, or services (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

Name, business name, email, phone, billing address, role, and credentials when you register.

B. Customer Data

Insurance scopes, supplement estimates, photos, customer names and contact info, claim numbers, addresses, project details, and other content uploaded by you or your authorized users. You own this data. We process it on your behalf to deliver the Service.

C. Payment Information

Payment details (credit card, ACH) are processed by our payment partners (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full card numbers.

D. Usage & Device Data

IP address, browser type, device, pages viewed, features used, timestamps, and similar telemetry, gathered automatically through cookies and analytics tools.

E. Communications

Emails, support tickets, and chat messages you send us, plus emails the Service sends through your account (e.g., adjuster emails) for delivery, history, and audit purposes.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide the Service. Process scopes, generate supplements, store data, and deliver functionality.
  • Improve the Service. Analyze aggregated and de-identified usage patterns to improve features and AI quality. We do not use your unaggregated Customer Data to train third-party AI models.
  • Billing. Process subscription fees, send receipts, manage refunds.
  • Security & fraud prevention. Detect abuse, prevent unauthorized access, audit access logs.
  • Communicate. Send transactional emails (account, billing, security), product updates, and (with consent) marketing.
  • Legal compliance. Comply with applicable law, respond to lawful requests, and enforce our agreements.

4. AI Processing

The Service uses AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) to process text and images you submit. Third-party AI providers act as our data sub-processors and are contractually bound not to use your data to train their general models. We do not send personally identifiable customer data to AI providers beyond what is necessary to perform the requested analysis.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only as follows:

  • Service providers / sub-processors (cloud hosting, AI providers, email delivery, payment processors) under contracts that require confidentiality and data security;
  • Within your organization — users at your company can access the same workspace;
  • Legal compliance — when required by valid legal process or to protect rights, property, or safety;
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice;
  • With your consent.

6. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, including encryption in transit, encryption at rest for sensitive fields, role-based access controls, audit logs, and regular backups. No system is 100% secure; you are responsible for protecting your account credentials.

7. Data Retention

We retain Customer Data while your subscription is active and for up to 90 days after termination, after which it may be deleted from active systems (subject to backup retention up to 12 months). You may export your data at any time during the active subscription period.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object to processing or request data portability. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@getclaimlogic.com. We will respond within applicable legal timeframes.

9. Children

The Service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Cookies

We use first-party cookies for authentication, session management, and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings; blocking essential cookies may break Service functionality.

11. International Transfers

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email or in-product notice at least 14 days before they take effect.
